## Description (max 4000 chars)

Libre Key Companion turns your iPhone into a manager for hardware security keys.
Tap your key to NFC or plug it into USB-C and work with everything it stores —
from a single, free, ad-free app.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• One-time passcodes (OATH) — list TOTP/HOTP credentials with live codes, add
  new ones by scanning a QR code or pasting an otpauth:// link, and delete the
  ones you no longer need. Works with the standard YKOATH applet used by
  YubiKey-class and compatible keys.

• Token2 on-device OTP — list, add, and remove TOTP entries stored directly on
  Token2 FIDO keys, including touch-required profiles.

• FIDO2 / passkeys — read your authenticator's details, set or change its PIN,
  toggle always-require-user-verification, and view or delete passkeys with full
  per-passkey details. (FIDO2 management is available over NFC.)

• PIV and OpenPGP — view certificate slots, key details, and PIN retry counters.

• FIDO metadata — resolve your key's model name, icon, and certification level
  (e.g. FIDO Certified L2) from the FIDO Alliance metadata service, with an
  in-app update to refresh the catalog.

TRANSPORTS

• NFC — works with every supported applet. Hold your key to the top of the phone.
• USB-C — read OATH, Token2 OTP, PIV, and OpenPGP by plugging the key in; the app
  detects it automatically. (FIDO2 management is NFC only on iOS.)

PRIVACY FIRST

No account. No analytics. No ads. No tracking. Codes and key data are shown on
screen only and never leave your device. The single optional network request
downloads the public FIDO Alliance metadata file and sends none of your data.

Libre Key Companion is provided by Token2.
